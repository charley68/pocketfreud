💡 Learn More About PocketFreud

Your gentle AI companion for emotional support, mood tracking, and private journaling — available 24/7 with no judgment or pressure

🎯 What Is PocketFreud?

PocketFreud is designed to offer a calming, intelligent presence when you need to reflect, talk, or just feel heard. It’s not a therapist — it’s a compassionate assistant helping you through your emotional ups and downs.

✨ Key Features

  • 😊 Mood tracking with visual charts and trends.
  • 📓 Private journaling with emotional summaries.
  • 🧠 Smart conversation with AI-driven reflections.
  • 🕊️ Light Chat mode for casual venting (nothing saved).
  • 🔐 No personal info required — stay anonymous if you choose.

🔒 Your Privacy Comes First

Your conversations are yours. PocketFreud stores minimal data, encrypted and never sold. You choose what to share, and you can delete anything at any time.
