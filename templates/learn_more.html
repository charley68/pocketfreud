PocketFreud is your AI companion for emotional support and mental well-being. Whether you're looking for someone to talk to, keep a journal, or reflect on your feelings, PocketFreud is here 24/7 — non-judgmental, private, and always listening.

Use it daily to track your mood, express your thoughts, or simply vent in a safe, supportive space. You can revisit your previous chats, set goals for emotional growth, or just check in with how you're feeling each day.

No diagnosis. No pressure. Just a helpful companion to make life's mental load feel a little lighter.
